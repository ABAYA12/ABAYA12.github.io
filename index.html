Omsu Legon Excellence Award 2024 (OLEA-2024)

  • Best OMSU Leader of the Year

    Awarded to the student who has shown exceptional leadership and commitment to the OMSU Legon.

  • Most Fashionable Student

    For the student who stands out with the most stylish and trendy fashion sense throughout the year.

  • Best Couple

    Recognizes the most admired couple for their relationship and contributions to the student community.

  • Best Student Single Lady or Man

    Awarded to the outstanding single student who has excelled academically, socially, or in extracurricular activities.

  • Best Student Entrepreneur of the Year

    For the student who has shown remarkable entrepreneurial skills and success in their business ventures.

  • Most Punctual Student

    Awarded to the student with the best track record for punctuality in all their commitments and activities.
